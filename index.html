Nearest Attractions

Gavi

Tucked within the Periyar Tiger Reserve, Gavi is a hidden eco-tourism haven known for misty forests, calm lakes, and exotic wildlife. Perfect for birdwatching, trekking, and nature lovers seeking peace and untouched wilderness.
📍 From Ranni: ~70 km | 2 hours

Konni Elephant Training Centre

This heritage center lets visitors observe and interact with elephants in a natural setting. Learn about traditional training practices, enjoy forest walks, and experience the cultural importance of elephants in Kerala’s history.
📍 From Ranni: ~30 km | 45 minutes

Perunthenaruvi Waterfalls

A scenic cascade on the Pamba River, Perunthenaruvi offers natural rock formations, lush greenery, and a relaxing ambiance. Ideal for quiet getaways, picnics, and clicking memorable nature shots.
📍 From Ranni: ~15 km | 30 minutes

Kakki Reservoir

Located inside dense forest, Kakki is a serene reservoir known for boating and wildlife sightings. A peaceful retreat where you can unwind surrounded by the beauty of nature and tall, green hills.
📍 From Ranni: ~45 km | 1.5 hours

Aranmula (Heritage Village & Boat Race)

Home to the famed snake boat race and Aranmula Kannadi (metal mirrors), this riverside village blends heritage, spiritual vibes, and traditional craftsmanship in one vibrant cultural package.
📍 From Ranni: ~25 km | 45 minutes

Kuttikkanam

A picturesque hill town with tea gardens, pine forests, and cool air. Ideal for refreshing retreats, scenic drives, and short hikes along rolling green hills and mist-kissed paths.
📍 From Ranni: ~55 km | 1.5 hours

Thekkady

A wildlife-rich paradise offering boat safaris, spice plantations, and trekking trails. Spot elephants, bison, and more in the Periyar Tiger Reserve while breathing in the crisp mountain air.
📍 From Ranni: ~90 km | 2.5 hours

Adavi Ecotourism & Kottavanchi Savari

Famous for bamboo rafting on the Kallar River, Adavi is a quiet forest spot perfect for nature lovers and families seeking a relaxing and rustic escape.
📍 From Ranni: ~27.7 km | 43 minutes

Vagamon

Misty meadows, pine valleys, and green tea estates define Vagamon. A calm hill station that offers adventure sports, romantic scenery, and a break from the lowland heat.
📍 From Ranni: ~67.5 km | 1 hour 53 minutes

Thenmala Ecotourism

India’s first planned eco-tourism site featuring canopy walks, boating, adventure zones, and music fountains. A great mix of nature, fun, and light thrill.
📍 From Ranni: ~80 km | 2 hours

Shendurney Wildlife Sanctuary

A biodiversity hotspot near Thenmala, this forest sanctuary is home to rare flora and fauna. Explore forest trails, boat across reservoirs, and enjoy the raw beauty of nature.
📍 From Ranni: ~85 km | 2 hours

Neendakara Fishing Harbor

A vibrant fishing village near Kollam. Watch the daily catch arrive, stroll the pier, or enjoy seafood at local eateries while soaking in coastal charm.
📍 From Ranni: ~105 km | 2.5 hours

Paippad Puthenpalli Church

A serene Roman Catholic church nestled in the heart of Paippad, known for its graceful architecture and peaceful surroundings. It serves as a spiritual haven for the local community and visitors alike.
📍 From Ranni: ~30 km | ~46 minutes​

Manjinikkara Church

A revered Syrian Christian pilgrimage site atop a hill, known for the tomb of Patriarch Ignatius Elias III. The church draws thousands during its annual feast and offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills.
📍 From Ranni: ~13 km | ~30 minutes

Veli Tourist Village

A scenic waterfront destination where the Veli Lake meets the Arabian Sea, offering pedal boating, floating cafés, and landscaped gardens ideal for family picnics and serene walks.
📍 From Ranni: ~120 km | ~3–3.5 hours
